Teenage Mom

…things happened…

I was a teenage mom that was found in the dumpster with my new litter of kittens by my mommy’s former coworker. She had taken taken all in and called me “Socks” but I always knew I was better than that. She took care of me and my litter for a little while and then, slowly, she found new homes for my kittens. Then all that was left was me and one of my kittens.

Next thing I knew, my last kitten and I were suddenly moving in with my mommy, but I remember her telling me that we just have to convince daddy to keep me. ‘Why wouldn’t he?’ I thought. I knew I was right when the first time he saw me and said I was beautiful.

The Big Sister Diva

…new brothers…

Soon after my new parents decided to adopt me and my baby, she became very sick and died. My mommy and daddy were sad, but my daddy said that I needed a “companion” to play with. I thought, ‘No I don’t. I’m purr-fectly fine it being just us, but they didn’t listen to me. Next thing I knew, they brought home my enemy, Obsidian, and the “special one,” Othello.

I know the truth those. They are actually my toys.

Years later, mommy moved away from daddy, but he still comes to see me. After a while, I thought we were good, but then she bought that energetic puppy, Washburn. I am not pleased with him, but he avoids me, I will let him stay.
